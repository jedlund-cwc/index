1. Theory of Operation

Mutual Induction

The ignition coil is an Autotransformer. It uses a small current in a primary circuit to create a massive magnetic field. When that circuit is broken, the field collapses across thousands of secondary windings.

"The rate of collapse determines the voltage. The faster the field drops, the higher the spike."

Internal Induction Logic

Primary (Input) 12V-14V
Turns Ratio 1:100
Secondary (Output) 40,000V+
